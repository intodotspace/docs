---
title: "Placing a Market Order"
---
Placing a market order is easy and best suited for those looking for a quick trade.

Step 1) Find a market outcome you wish to buy

Step 2) Choose either YES or NO to predict the market and make sure ‘Market’ is selected at the top right corner

Step 3) Input how much you’d like to buy in $USDC, you will then see how many shares this will amount to as well as your potential return.

Step 3) Click Buy

You can then choose to wait until the market is resolved and either YES or NO answers are marked as victorious. 

If you wish to exit your position earlier than the market’s resolution, follow the steps below:

Step 1) Go to Portfolio at the top of your screen

Step 2) Open the market you wish to exit and click ‘Sell’

Step 3) You can then click the ‘Max’ button to sell all your shares

Step 4) Click ‘Sell’
