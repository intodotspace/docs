---
title: "Managing Orders and Positions"
---
You can view and manage your positions, orders and history by clicking ‘Portfolio’ at the top of the page. 

This page will show your total portfolio balance, cash, profit/lloss as well as the below. 

**Positions**
- For each of your positions, you can see the market, price at which entered, your bet, your bet’s current value, profit/loss percentage as well as options to sell your position or share your profit/loss card. 


**Open Orders**
- You can close your orders in the ‘Open Orders’ tab by clicking the X button on the right of each order 

**History**
- Here you can see the history of your closed trades and performance
