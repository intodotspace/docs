---
title: "Point System"
---

Points are an in-platform currency earned through engagement and participation that unlock exclusive benefits. The more you use Space, the more you can rank up and earn.

![Points Pn](/images/points.png)

## Earning Points

You can earn points through numerous ways:

- Trading activity
- Daily logins and streak bonuses
- Task completion
- Participating in competitions
- Referrals
- Community participation
- Engagement on social platforms
- Unlocking achievements

## Using Points

Points unlock special benefits:

- Earning \$SPACE airdrops
- Invitations to special events
- Trading on special Points Markets
- Testing strategies without financial risk
- Building reputation and ranking up in leaderboards
