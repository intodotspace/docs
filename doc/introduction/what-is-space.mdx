title: "What is Space?"
---

Space is a decentralized prediction market platform built on Solana where you can bet on real-world outcomes across crypto, politics, sports, technology, culture, and more - and earn rewards for being right.

## What Makes Space Different

  <Card title="True Price Discovery" icon="bullseye" iconType="solid" color="#5EDD2C">

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a market" titleSize="h3">
    Crypto prices, election outcomes, sports results, tech launches, cultural moments and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place your bet" titleSize="h3">
    Buy YES or NO shares based on what you think will happen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get rewarded" titleSize="h3">
    If you're right, each of your winning shares pays you out \$1. Exit early if the market moves in your favor and you want to cash out earlier.
  </Step>
</Steps>
