## General Support

For general questions, technical issues, or account assistance, contact us at [support@into.space](mailto:support@into.space) and a situation-appropriate team member will get back to you.

## Community Support

Join our channel and community on Telegram for real-time help and discussions:

- **Telegram (Ann):** [t.me/intodotspace](https://t.me/intodotspace)
- **Telegram (Chat):** [t.me/spacechat](https://t.me/spacechat)

Our team members are active and ready to help.

<Warning>
  **Watch out for scams and impersonators!** Space team members will never DM you first or ask for your private keys, seed phrases or funds.
</Warning>

## Developer Support

Building on Space or integrating with our API? Reach out to [developers@into.space](mailto:developers@into.space) for help or check our [developer documentation](/build/overview) for technical resources.
